2.7 million newborns die annually within the first 28 days of birth, globally

Majority are avoidable with improved healthcare, preventing brain injury of the baby during labour and delivery

VISION
A future where childbirth is safer for babies and mums.
MISSION
Prevent injury to babies during labour and delivery through development and implementation of innovative technologies for timely clinical decision-support and fetal monitoring.
GOAL
Secure regulatory approval and get our Fit for Labour technology adopted by more than 5 hospitals in the UK by 2026.
